Welcome to Fiona's Fineries online.
Fiona's Fineries is an Historical Clothing Company,
specializing in Renaissance Garb. Our booth can be
found at Renaissance Faires all over the country,
with base camp being in Kirtland, OH.

What is our quest? Not to seek the holy grail,
but to provide comfortable, pretty and affordable garb
(NOT costumes) that the discerning
Patron or Participant of Ren Faires
and SCA events can wear, beat up, get dirty,
sweat in and in general live in for days on end,
then throw in the washer (or in some cases, dry clean)
and repeat the whole process.

It's out mission to prove that bodices and corsets,
if made and worn correctly are comfortable and a
joy to wear and be seen in. And secretly,
it is our hope to see every single
attendee of Ren Events wearing our clothes.
